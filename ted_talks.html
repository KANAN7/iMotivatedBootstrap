Top 10 TED Talks That Will Change Your Life

1. The Power of Vulnerability - Brené Brown

Brené Brown explores the importance of vulnerability, connection, and authenticity in achieving personal growth.

2. Do Schools Kill Creativity? - Sir Ken Robinson

Sir Ken Robinson makes a compelling case for creating an education system that nurtures creativity rather than stifling it.

3. Your Body Language May Shape Who You Are - Amy Cuddy

Amy Cuddy discusses how body language affects how others see us and how we see ourselves, showing how "power posing" can improve confidence.

4. Inside the Mind of a Master Procrastinator - Tim Urban

Tim Urban takes us on a hilarious journey through YouTube binges, Wikipedia rabbit holes, and bouts of staring out the window — and encourages us to think harder about what we’re really procrastinating on, before we run out of time.

5. How Great Leaders Inspire Action - Simon Sinek

Simon Sinek explores how leaders can inspire cooperation, trust, and change, explaining the Golden Circle concept.
